ALEXSAAS NEW HIRE COMPLIANCE CHECKLIST

REQUIRED FOR ALL HIRES:
1. Background check (completed before start date via Checkr)
2. I-9 Employment Eligibility Verification (within 3 business days)
3. W-4 Federal Tax Withholding
4. State tax withholding forms
5. NDA and IP Assignment Agreement
6. Employee Handbook acknowledgment (signed)
7. IT Security Policy acknowledgment (signed)
8. Anti-harassment training (within 30 days)
9. Data privacy training (within 30 days)
10. Direct deposit enrollment

CALIFORNIA-SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS:
- Pay transparency: Salary range must be disclosed in job posting
- Sexual harassment prevention training: 2 hours within 6 months
- Notice to Employee (Labor Code Section 2810.5)
- Cal/OSHA Injury Prevention Program acknowledgment
- Paid Family Leave notice

NEW YORK-SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS:
- NY Paid Family Leave notice
- Sexual harassment prevention training: Annual requirement
- NYC salary transparency compliance
- Workers compensation notice

INTERNATIONAL HIRES:
- Work visa verification
- Tax treaty documentation
- Country-specific employment contract
- Local labor law compliance review

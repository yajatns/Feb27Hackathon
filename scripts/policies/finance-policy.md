# Finance & Expense Policy — Alex SaaS Inc

*Effective Date: January 1, 2026 | Version 4.1 | Policy Owner: CFO*

## 1. Expense Limits & Categories

### 1.1 Per-Transaction Limits (No Pre-Approval Required)

| Category | Individual Limit | Notes |
|----------|-----------------|-------|
| Meals (business) | $75/person | Client meals: $150/person max |
| Office supplies | $100/transaction | Recurring orders need manager approval |
| Software/subscriptions | $50/month | Over $50 → IT procurement process |
| Books & learning materials | $200/transaction | Part of $5K L&D annual budget |
| Team events | $50/person | Manager-organized, max 1/month |
| Transportation (local) | $75/trip | Uber/Lyft for business meetings |

### 1.2 Pre-Approval Required

| Category | Threshold | Approver |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| Any single expense | > $500 | Direct manager |
| Any single expense | > $2,500 | Department head |
| Any single expense | > $10,000 | CFO |
| Any single expense | > $50,000 | CEO + CFO |
| Recurring commitment | > $1,000/month | Department head + Finance |
| New vendor engagement | Any amount | Procurement + Legal review |

## 2. Approval Thresholds & Workflow

### 2.1 Purchase Order (PO) Requirements
- **PO required** for all purchases > $5,000.
- **3 competitive bids** required for purchases > $25,000.
- **Board notification** required for commitments > $100,000.
- **Board approval** required for commitments > $500,000.

### 2.2 Approval Chain
```
Employee → Manager → Department Head → Finance Controller → CFO → CEO → Board
         ($500)      ($2,500)          ($10,000)          ($50K)  ($100K) ($500K)
```

### 2.3 Emergency Purchases
- Up to $5,000 without pre-approval if documented within 48 hours.
- Must be tagged as "emergency" in Expensify with written justification.
- Manager must retroactively approve within 5 business days.

## 3. Travel Policy

### 3.1 Air Travel
- **Domestic flights:** Economy class. Business class permitted for flights > 6 hours.
- **International flights:** Business class for flights > 8 hours. First class never reimbursed.
- Book through corporate travel portal (Navan). Personal booking reimbursed at corporate rate only.
- Book at least 14 days in advance when possible; last-minute premium requires manager approval.
- Frequent flyer miles earned on business travel belong to the employee.

### 3.2 Hotels
- **Maximum nightly rate by city:**
  - San Francisco / New York City: $350/night
  - Seattle / Austin / Boston / LA / Chicago: $275/night
  - All other US cities: $200/night
  - International: equivalent of $300 USD/night
- Extended stays (5+ nights): negotiate corporate rate or use Airbnb (with manager approval).

### 3.3 Ground Transportation
- Rideshare (Uber/Lyft): standard tier for solo, XL permitted for groups of 3+.
- Rental cars: compact/mid-size; SUV permitted for 3+ travelers. Always purchase insurance waiver.
- Personal vehicle: IRS standard mileage rate ($0.70/mile for 2026).

### 3.4 Meals During Travel
- Per diem: $75/day domestic, $100/day international (receipts not required for per diem).
- Alternatively, actual expense with receipts (cannot mix per diem and actual in same trip).
- Alcohol: max $30/person for business dinners. No reimbursement for personal consumption.

### 3.5 Travel Approval
- Domestic travel: manager approval.
- International travel: department head + Finance approval.
- Conference attendance: manager approval + $3,000 max (including registration, travel, hotel).
- All travel booked 14+ days in advance where possible.

## 4. Reimbursement

### 4.1 Submission Requirements
- Submit expenses within **30 days** of incurrence. Expenses > 60 days old are not reimbursable.
- Receipt required for all expenses > $25.
- Itemized receipts required for meals (no summary receipts).
- Business purpose documented for every expense.

### 4.2 Processing Timeline
- Expenses submitted by Friday → processed by following Wednesday.
- Reimbursement paid via direct deposit in next payroll cycle.
- Expedited reimbursement (within 5 business days) available for expenses > $1,000.

### 4.3 Non-Reimbursable Expenses
- Personal entertainment, clothing, gym memberships.
- Traffic violations, parking tickets.
- First-class air travel.
- Mini-bar charges.
- Spouse/partner travel expenses (unless pre-approved for company event).
- Personal cell phone bills (covered by remote work stipend).
- Gifts > $50/person (require manager + HR approval).

## 5. Budget Owner Rules

### 5.1 Budget Ownership
- Every cost center has a designated **Budget Owner** (typically department head).
- Budget Owners are responsible for: monthly spend tracking, variance reporting, forecasting.
- Budget Owners receive automated alerts at 75% and 90% of monthly budget.

### 5.2 Budget Allocation
- Annual budget set during Q4 planning cycle (October–November).
- Quarterly reforecast in weeks 2-3 of each quarter.
- Budget transfers between cost centers require CFO approval.
- Unspent budget does NOT roll over to next quarter.

### 5.3 Headcount Budget
- Each approved headcount includes: base salary + 30% burden (benefits, taxes, equipment).
- Open headcount budget released after 120 days if position unfilled.
- Backfill within 10% of departed employee's total comp: auto-approved.
- Backfill > 10% above: requires Finance + HR approval.

### 5.4 Vendor Management
- All vendors > $10K annual spend require a signed MSA reviewed by Legal.
- Annual vendor review for all vendors > $50K spend.
- Payment terms: Net 30 standard, Net 45 for vendors > $100K (negotiated).
- No auto-renewal clauses without Finance awareness (flag in contract review).

## 6. Corporate Card Policy

### 6.1 Eligibility
- Corporate Amex issued to L5+ employees and all managers.
- Virtual cards available for one-time purchases (any employee, via Ramp).
- Shared department cards: controlled by Budget Owner, reconciled monthly.

### 6.2 Card Usage Rules
- Business expenses only — no personal charges.
- Monthly reconciliation required by 5th of following month.
- Unreconciled charges after 45 days: escalated to manager, then payroll deduction.
- Card suspended after 2 consecutive months of late reconciliation.

### 6.3 Spending Controls (Ramp)
- Auto-categorization of transactions.
- Real-time receipt matching via mobile app.
- Merchant category blocking: no gambling, personal retail, cash advances.
- Per-transaction and monthly limits set per cardholder by Finance.

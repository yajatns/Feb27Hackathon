# Human Resources Policy — Alex SaaS Inc

*Effective Date: January 1, 2026 | Version 3.2 | Policy Owner: VP of People*

## 1. Hiring Process

### 1.1 Requisition & Approval
- All open positions require a signed Job Requisition Form approved by: hiring manager → department head → Finance (headcount budget).
- Requisitions expire after 90 days if unfilled; extension requires re-approval.
- Internal transfers: employee must have 12+ months in current role and manager approval.

### 1.2 Interview Process
- **Phone Screen** (30 min) — recruiter fit check.
- **Technical / Functional Round** (60 min) — hiring manager + 1 team member.
- **Culture & Values Round** (45 min) — cross-functional interviewer.
- **Executive Round** (30 min) — required for L5+ roles or salary > $180K.
- Offer decision within 5 business days of final interview.

### 1.3 Background & Reference Checks
- All offers contingent on: criminal background check, employment verification (last 5 years), education verification.
- Engineering roles: additional code plagiarism check on take-home.
- Finance roles: credit check required (with candidate consent).

## 3. Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)

### 3.1 PIP Criteria
- Triggered after 2 consecutive "Does Not Meet Expectations" reviews, or manager-initiated with HR approval.
- Duration: 30 days (IC roles), 45 days (management roles).

### 3.2 PIP Process
1. Manager documents specific performance gaps with measurable targets.
2. HR reviews and approves PIP document.
3. Employee receives PIP in 1:1 meeting with manager + HRBP present.
4. Weekly check-ins during PIP period.
5. Final assessment: either "PIP Passed" (return to normal) or "PIP Failed" (proceed to termination).

### 3.3 PIP Restrictions
- No PIP during first 90 days (use probation off-boarding instead).
- Employee on approved medical/family leave: PIP paused until return.
- Maximum 1 PIP per employee per 12-month period.

## 4. Termination

### 4.1 Voluntary Termination
- 2-week notice standard; 4-week notice for Director+ roles.
- Exit interview conducted by HR within final 3 days.
- Stock vesting stops on last day; 90-day exercise window for vested options.

### 4.2 Involuntary Termination
- Requires written approval: manager → department head → HR → Legal.
- Severance: 2 weeks base pay per year of service (minimum 4 weeks, maximum 26 weeks).
- COBRA continuation offered for 3 months (company-paid).
- All equipment must be returned within 5 business days.

### 4.3 Cause-Based Termination (Immediate)
- Grounds: fraud, theft, harassment, data breach, violence, substance abuse on premises.
- No severance for cause-based termination.
- Legal review required within 24 hours of incident.

## 5. Benefits Eligibility

### 5.1 Health Insurance
- Eligible from first day of employment (no waiting period).
- Plans: PPO (company pays 90% of premium), HMO (company pays 95%), HSA-eligible HDHP (company contributes $1,500/yr to HSA).
- Dental and Vision included in all plans.

### 5.2 401(k)
- Eligible after 30 days. Company match: 100% up to 4% of salary.
- Immediate vesting on employee contributions; 2-year cliff vesting on employer match.

### 5.3 Paid Time Off
- **PTO:** Unlimited for L5+ (minimum 15 days encouraged). L3–L4: 20 days/year accrued.
- **Sick Leave:** 10 days/year (unused rolls over, max 30 days banked).
- **Parental Leave:** 16 weeks primary caregiver, 8 weeks secondary caregiver (fully paid).
- **Bereavement:** 5 days immediate family, 3 days extended family.

### 5.4 Other Benefits
- $5,000/year learning & development stipend.
- $500/month remote work stipend (for fully remote employees).
- Mental health: 12 free therapy sessions/year via EAP.
- Commuter benefits: pre-tax transit up to IRS maximum.

ALEXSAAS EXPENSE & TRAVEL POLICY

DAILY LIMITS (Business Travel):
- Meals: Up to $75/day
- Hotels: Up to $250/night (major cities: $350/night)
- Ground transport: Reasonable (Uber/Lyft preferred over rental)
- Flights: Economy for flights under 4 hours, Business for 4+ hours

CLIENT ENTERTAINMENT:
- Up to $150/person
- Pre-approval required for expenses over $500
- Must include business purpose and attendee list

SOFTWARE & TOOLS:
- Under $50/month: Self-serve, submit receipt
- $50-$500/month: Manager approval required
- Over $500/month: Director approval + procurement review

APPROVAL THRESHOLDS:
- Under $500: Self-approved (submit receipt within 5 days)
- $500-$2,000: Manager approval
- $2,000-$10,000: Director approval
- Over $10,000: VP/C-suite approval

TEAM EVENTS:
- Up to $100/person/quarter for team outings
- Annual team offsite: $500/person budget
- Holiday party: Company-funded

REIMBURSEMENT:
- Submit within 30 days of expense
- Original receipts required for all expenses over $25
- Processing time: 5-7 business days
- Corporate card holders: Reconcile within 5 business days
